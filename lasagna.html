Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic italian dish, with ooey-goeey tomato sauce and cheese.

Perfect comfort food for any winter day.

Ingredients

  • 1Kg Ground Beef
  • 300g Mozarella
  • 150g Ricotta
  • 15 Sheets of Lasagna Pasta, boiled
  • San Marzano Tomato sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in water until softened but pliable
  2. Place tomato sauce on bottom of pan
  3. Add ricotta and mozarella
  4. Place a third of the beef on the same layer
  5. Add more pasta layers
  6. Repeat
  7. Finish top with all cheese and optional breadcrumbs for a crust
